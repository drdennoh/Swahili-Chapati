Welcome to my site about Swahili Chapati recipe

The chapati is a traditional bread brought by the Indian tradition, but then evolved throughout the country and today prepared on the street, in poor places as well as in those of high level as an accompaniment for dishes. Its simple and quick preparation makes it a tasty and filling dish to be paired with beans, vegetables or meat dishes and soups.

Ingredients

  • Baking Flour - 350g
    • Make it exact amount
  • Sugar - 15g
  • Salt - 15g
  • Vegetable oil - 30ml

Steps

  1. Sieve the flour,add sugar,salt and oil.

    😚

  2. Mix the ingredients then add lukewarm water.
    😆
  3. Knead the dough until soft without lumps.
    😪
  4. Make around 15 balls. 😝
  5. Roll the balls to form a circular shape. 😧
  6. Shallow fry in a hot pan using medium heat. 😰

The ultimate product of chapati recipe is very soft and smells nice to your nose. It has a delicious but subtle flavor that is why it can be easily paired with almost any dish whether sweet or savory making it an extremely versatile.
